I remember marveling at the colors and landscapes of the comics and my school books

Painting on a canvas, on paper or on any surface that allows us to express ourselves can be a way to express feelings and thoughts. Especially for people who need to communicate them and are not able to do it by other means. In addition, I consider that it is a type of alternative and natural therapy that can have many benefits.
